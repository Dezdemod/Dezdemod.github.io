Blackjack

About


- Try to get a higher hand value than the dealer's without going over 21 in a simple Blackjack game with expected win/lose conditions such as higher cards, opponent is bust and draw between opponents.

- Dealer always reveals his second card when the player stands.

- The game detects if the player or dealer has a blackjack at the start of the game so you don't need to wait until the end of the round to find out who the winner is.
